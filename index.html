WHO WE ARE

AVGVSTO is an open-source project designed to provide hardware-oriented advanced encryption solutions. Uses your USB drive as a physical key to ensure only you can access your files.

Ideal for developers, businesses, or privacy-conscious individuals who want maximum protection for sensitive data. Each encrypted file is bound to your USB's unique identifier and requires a strong passphrase.
